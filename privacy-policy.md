Skill Catalyst we respect your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This

By using the app, you agree to the collection and use of your information as described in this Privacy
Policy.
1. Information We Collect
When you register for our app, we collect the following personal information:
• Username
• Email Address
• Password
We may also collect data such as your device's information for authentication purposes, but we do
not collect any additional personal or sensitive data.
2. How We Use Your Information
We use the collected information for the following purposes:
• Account creation and authentication: To allow you to register and log in to the app.
• Providing services: To facilitate the peer-to-peer skill exchange through chat and video calls.

• Security: To secure user accounts and protect your data from unauthorized access.
3. Data Storage and Security
Your data, including account credentials, is securely stored in Firebase Realtime Database. We take
appropriate security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access,

We use VideoSDK to provide video call functionality and screen sharing during calls. This service may

VideoSDK’s privacy practices can be found in their own privacy policy, which we encourage you to
review.

We do not share your personal information with third parties, except as required by law or to protect

6. Consent
By using the app, you consent to the collection and use of your personal information as described in
this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with our policy, please do not use the app.
7. Children’s Privacy
Our app is intended for adults, specifically computer engineers aged 12 and older. We do not
knowingly collect personal data from individuals under the age of 12. If we become aware that we
have inadvertently collected information from a child under the age of 12, we will take steps to
delete that information.
8. Cookies and Tracking Technologies
We do not use cookies or any tracking technologies in our app. The only data stored is your login
credentials for easy access to your account.
